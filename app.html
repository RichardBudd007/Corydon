VAT Reclaim

HMRC VAT431NB — submit within 3 months of practical completion

How it works
Your new build is zero-rated. You can reclaim 20% VAT on building materials you buy directly yourself using HMRC form VAT431NB. You cannot reclaim VAT on contractor invoices (Brindley James). Keep every receipt. Submit within 3 months of practical completion. The form is at gov.uk/claim-vat-new-home.
