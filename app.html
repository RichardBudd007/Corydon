How new build VAT reclaim works
Your new build is zero-rated for VAT. You can reclaim 20% VAT on building materials you buy yourself directly (from builders merchants, suppliers etc.) using HMRC form VAT431NB. You cannot reclaim VAT on contractor invoices — their labour is already zero-rated. Keep every receipt. Submit within 3 months of completion.
